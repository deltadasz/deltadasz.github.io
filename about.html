About me


My name is Jakab Andrada. I was born in small town in Transilvania, where I finished art high school. For me becoming a fashion designer was a childhood dream. I've managed to reach that dream in 2015 when I was admitted at the Cluj Napoca Art School where i finished fashion design in 2018. I had the opportunity to work with a fashion design company in Budapest, and I have participated at many fashion shows at different festivals.
